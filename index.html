Privacy Policy for Spenzo

Last Updated: September 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Spenzo ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information, especially the financial data you input.

Crucial Note on Data Storage:

This is a local-only application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your financial transactions or related sensitive data on any external servers. **Your data stays securely on your device.**

1. Local-Only Data Storage and Security (Your Expense Data)

The core purpose of the App is to provide secure, private expense tracking.

A. Data Stored Locally

  • **Data Stored:** All financial data, including transactions, categories, budgets, and calculation results ("Local Data"), is stored securely within the local storage of your mobile device.
  • **No Server Transmission:** We do not access, view, copy, or transmit your Local Data to any external servers, third parties, or cloud services operated by us.
  • **Data Deletion:** Because the Local Data resides only on your device, deleting the App will result in the **permanent deletion of all your Local Data.** We have no ability to recover this data once the App is uninstalled.

B. Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure that your Local Data is protected on your device using secure local storage mechanisms appropriate to the platform (e.g., encryption where supported by the operating system).

2. Information We Collect (Required and Optional Personal Data)

While the financial data remains local, the App requires a username for setup and may optionally collect an email address for the following non-sensitive information:

Data Type Purpose How it is Collected Retention
**Name or Username** (Required) To create your unique user profile, personalize the in-app experience, and for user identification in support communications. Collected during the initial setup or registration process. Retained locally on your device.
**Email Address** (Optional) To respond to support inquiries, receive bug reports, or send occasional, non-marketing updates if you subscribe. When you manually submit it via a support form or email link inside the App. Retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose, or until you request deletion.

You are required to provide a Name or Username during setup. You are not required to provide an email address to use the core expense tracking features of the App.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses minimal third-party services, primarily for operational reasons:

  • **Analytics:** We use **None**. We do not use any third-party analytics that track user behavior or send data off-device. This decision reinforces our commitment to user privacy.
  • **App Store Providers:** Apple App Store and Google Play Store collect certain data (like download count and geographic location) when you download the App. This is governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • **No Advertising:** We do not currently use third-party advertising partners that would collect data for the purpose of serving targeted ads.

5. Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA)

Since we do not collect or transmit your financial data, most user rights related to accessing or correcting data held by a company do not apply to your Local Data.

  • **Right of Access and Correction (Local Data):** You can access, review, and modify all your Local Data and your Name/Username directly within the App at any time.
  • **Right of Deletion (Local Data):** You can delete all Local Data instantly by uninstalling the App from your device.
  • **Right of Deletion (Optional Personal Data):** If you have provided us with an email address for support, you may request its deletion at any time by contacting us using the information below.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
